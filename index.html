Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

The timeline depends on the project scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while a complex web application could take 3-6 months. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline.

What is your design process?

My design process follows a user-centered approach: Discovery & Research → Wireframing → Prototyping → UI Design → Development → Testing → Launch. I involve clients at every stage to ensure the final product meets their vision.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

Yes, I offer ongoing maintenance packages that include updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates. We can customize a package based on your specific needs.

What technologies do you specialize in?

My core stack includes React, Vue.js, Node.js, and MongoDB for development, and Figma, Adobe XD, and Sketch for design. I also work with various CSS frameworks, state management libraries, and deployment platforms.
